Calculus

Introduction

Calculus is the study of continuous change.

It is a fundamental area of mathematics that has numerous applications in science, engineering, economics, and other fields.

Limits and Continuity

A limit is the value that a function approaches as the input gets arbitrarily close to a certain point.

Continuity is the property of a function being continuous at a point, meaning that the function can be drawn without lifting the pencil from the paper.

Differentiation

Differentiation is the process of finding the derivative of a function.

The derivative represents the rate of change of the function with respect to the input.

Integration

Integration is the process of finding the integral of a function.

The integral represents the accumulation of the function over a defined interval.
